How to Play Energy Grid

Game Objective

Score the most points by creating fusions and eliminating opponent cores.

Turn Structure

  1. Place one Energy Core (required)
  2. Place any number of Charged Cores (optional, based on your available supply)
  3. Click "End Turn" to confirm placement

Placing Cores

Click on empty grid cells to select them for placement. Click again to deselect. Cores are placed at the end of your turn.

Fusions

When you have two matching cores adjacent to each other (horizontally or vertically), they fuse, earning you points and a new Charged Core.

Eliminations

Higher value cores will eliminate adjacent lower value enemy cores at the end of your turn, potentially causing chain reactions.

Scoring

  • Basic Fusion (2 cores): 2 points
  • Charged Core in fusion: +2 points extra
  • Eliminating Energy Core: 1 point
  • Eliminating Charged Core: 3 points

Game End

The game ends when a player reaches 20 points.
